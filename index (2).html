The Problem
Climate risk is already
a capital problem
In Florida, insurance premiums have doubled or tripled in a single renewal cycle. Insurers are pulling out entirely. Properties that penciled out three years ago no longer do — and most family offices have no system to see it coming. — From ValueGuard's Customer Discovery Research, 2025
🌊

Insurance exits accelerating

State Farm and Allstate pulled back aggressively from California wildfire zones and coastal Florida markets in 2025, leaving family offices without viable coverage options.

📉

NOI eroded by weather costs

Cap rates compress even as rents hold steady when insurance costs spike. The financial math on coastal real estate has quietly changed — permanently.

🔍

Existing tools weren't built for you

Platforms like Jupiter Intelligence and ClimateCheck were built for institutional risk researchers. A family office principal managing $200M doesn't need 2080 projections — they need next year's insurance renewal modeled into their NOI.

⚠️

Risk is handled reactively, not proactively

Customer discovery interviews confirmed: climate risk surfaces only when a renewal comes in — not when deals are evaluated or portfolios are reviewed.

How It Works
From address to decision brief in minutes

A single platform that centralizes climate intelligence, connects it to your portfolio, and fits how family offices actually make decisions.

01

Input any property address

Enter an address with basic financial assumptions. ValueGuard locates and loads all relevant climate exposure data automatically.

02

AI generates a Decision Brief

Flood, wildfire, hurricane, and sea-level exposure scores are translated into insurance cost trajectories, NOI sensitivity, and valuation impact.

03

Aggregate across your portfolio

All properties roll up into a real-time portfolio dashboard. See total exposure, concentration by peril, and how your risk profile is evolving over time.

04

Plan, monitor, and collaborate

Run what-if scenarios before acquisitions. Set alerts for threshold breaches. Share analyses with your advisors and investment committee — all in one place.

Platform Modules
Every tool your team needs to manage climate risk

ValueGuard is not a report you file away. It's an operational system your team uses daily — embedded into how you evaluate deals, review your portfolio, and plan for renewals.

📄

Climate-Adjusted Decision Brief

The platform's core output: a concise, AI-generated analysis of any property that translates climate exposure into insurance cost trajectories, NOI impact, valuation sensitivity, and refinancing feasibility. Formatted for investment committee materials.

🗺️

Portfolio Risk Aggregation

Aggregate property-level risk across all holdings. Visualize total exposure, geographic concentration, and climate peril distribution on a live geospatial heatmap.

🔮

Scenario Planning

Model what-if scenarios by adjusting insurance cost escalation, NOI assumptions, mitigation investments, and climate severity. See which assets are most impacted — before you commit.

🔔

Custom Alerts & Monitoring

Set threshold-based alerts for risk score changes, insurance cost projections, or financial metric breaches. ValueGuard becomes your early-warning system — not just a point-in-time tool.

👥

Team Collaboration

Shared access, commenting, and versioned reports for principals, investment team members, and external advisors. No more files passed back and forth.

🤖

AI-Powered Help & Import

An AI assistant handles platform queries and analysis questions. Import existing property data from CSV or Excel — the AI parses and maps it to your portfolio automatically.
